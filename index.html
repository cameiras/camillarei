About Camilla Rei

Camilla Rei curates unique collections of clothes, shoes, bags and accessories by Italian brands that define the style and taste of Italian women. These brands are hard to find outside of Italian boutiques, where the shopping experience is often enhanced by the magical touch of the boutique’s owner suggesting the best pairings and looks. We want to make this experience accessible outside of Italy, and we want our customers to feel unique, beautiful and timeless.

Our Story

We’re two Italian women who moved to the US over 10 years ago, and have been looking for a way to simplify their clothing shopping process, usually involving yearly trips to Italy to visit their families, and their favorite clothing shops. We created Camilla Rei to offer a curated collection of our most favorite brands, and to make it accessible online through a sleek and convenient online store.
